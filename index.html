MEET YOUR DEVELOPER

MICHAEL YANG is a developer, graphic artist, filmmaker, and photographer. Altogether, he has over ten years of experience in digital media content production, having worked for film festivals, local city government, political campaigns, and non-profit organizations. Outside of work, Yang enjoys reading, writing, and brazilian jiu-jitsu. Additionally, Yang is a fan of locally crafted beers, good food, better company, some fresh air and lots of sunshine.
